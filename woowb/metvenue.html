Conference Venue

The main program will be held at the Grand Ballroom, 23 rd Floor, Bangkok Convention Centre, Centara Hotel at CentralWorld The Centara Grand at CentralWorld Hotel is one of Asia Pacific's largest hotels, with over 500 rooms and suites, all fully equipped with modern amenities to ensure the most comfortable stay. The hotel is located within Bangkok's central business district, connected to CentralWorld, Asia Pacific's largest lifestyle living mall with over 500 stores and 50 restaurants.

Endoscopy venue

Olympus T-TEC (Thai-Training & Education Centre) is the 4th training centre established by Olympus in Asia Pacific outside Japan with the rest; 3 centers in China; Shanghai, Beijing and Guangzhou, and the latest one in South Korea that will be operated in October 2017. T-TEC has been operated since May 2016. The center is conveniently situated in Gemopolis Industrial Estate, Bangna – just 15 minutes from Bangkok Suvarnabhumi International Airport that is easily linked to the city centre.


T-TEC occupies 3 floors and a total space of 4,900 m2 complete with;

  • - Training rooms
  • - 92-seat auditorium equipped with 4K and HD 3D projector and audio systems
  • - Dry lab for up to 10 stations
  • - Wet lab with 9 stations equipped with HD 3D, 4K, Endo-Lap imaging,
    Thunderbeat and Laparoscopic, EndoUrology, ENT and Gastroenterology scopes and instruments.

T-TEC is well equipped for gastroenterology, laparoscopic and other minimally invasive surgical training.
